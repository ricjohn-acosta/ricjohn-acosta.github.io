Preparing for bootcamp.. (actually my final week reflections)

2/03/2020

What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning? I learned that I am patient, ask a lot of questions and I take my time when stuck with a problem.

What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming? To me, when it comes to learning and programming. We always need to be self-aware that it is okay to get stuck at something and it is normal to feel frustrated when it comes to programming. When teaching someone, empathy has a really big role because one needs to understand that everyone has their own style of learning.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning? The advantages learning how to learn gives you.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning? Telling myself to do the core exercises even though I don't feel like it.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course? Traditional schools does not teach you how to be a good person and how to prime yourself to learn programming.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead? Justify your answer. Not really but definitely more emphasis on programming.
