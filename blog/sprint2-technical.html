What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

27/11/19

I've always been confused between the three so as I was trying to learn these three things I have finally made my own analogy to remember these concepts: The hamburger analogy. Basically: Margin = Your hands holding the burger. Border = The buns. Padding = The toppings. (Cheese, lettuce, bacon) Content = The patty.
