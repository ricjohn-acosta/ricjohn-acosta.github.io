Preparing for bootcamp.. (actually my final week reflections)

2/03/2020

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway? My long term goal still hasn't changed. I still want to work as a software developer at some company.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations were in Bootcamp? I have to say that the biggest strength is my drive to reach my goal which is becoming a software developer. As for my biggest limitation, I'd say that my time management and discipline when it comes to taking a break from coding.

What did you think your biggest non-technical challenge was at Bootcamp? Asking for help and asking the right questions when needing help. Saving money during the course was also hard.

What non-technical skills - human skills - did you develop while in EDA? Being more empathatic to others, patience and I also learned to not compare myself to others too much as everyone learns at their own pace.

Did you reach your expectations that you set to yourself in the beginning of bootcamp? Tech wise, yes. I still need to get me a job though. :) Human-skills wise, I did not have much expectations but I've actually learned so much about myself and others during the course.
